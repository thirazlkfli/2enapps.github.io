Our Story

From Local Roots to Global Ambitions

2en Apps Sdn. Bhd. (2en Apps) is a local technology services company with a vision for global presence. Incorporated on 6 May 2011, we were founded by a group of experienced professionals from various industries specializing in ICT services and product delivery.

Born from the idea to bring a local company to the global stage, we believe that although our footprints are currently small, one day we will stand among the technology leaders.

We offer a comprehensive range of professional services including custom-made system development, hardware installation, and consultancy. We are also deeply committed to downstream activities such as System Design Consultancy and Project Management.
